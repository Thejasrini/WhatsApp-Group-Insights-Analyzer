5. Communication Style

The communication style within the group was primarily informational with distinct roles among participants:

  • Informational Broadcasts: Most messages were announcements rather than sustained discussions
  • Role-based Communication:
    • Sf Mangesh Baskar Sir served as the key source of technical information
    • Sf Kalpanjay Nathe handled event coordination and disseminated meeting details
    • Participants like Far Kiran Ramnath Waghchaure provided positive feedback
  • Limited Interaction: Minimal back-and-forth conversation; most interactions were acknowledgments
  • Multimodal Communication: Used text, documents, images, and audio files for information sharing
Communication Insight: The group functions more as an information distribution channel than a discussion forum, with clear role differentiation among participants.
